Current research labs I’m in

Outside Brandeis

Collaborative work with faculty at Jackson State University, Texas A&M, and UT Arlington on machine learning applications in computational fluid dynamics (CFD).

Inside Brandeis

Senior honors thesis modeling basal ganglia transitions from goal-directed to habitual learning. Using behavioral data on mice, differential-equation–based models and Reinforcment Learning tools, I aim to capture how dopamine-dependent signals couple the dynamics of the dorsomedial and dorsolateral striatum.
